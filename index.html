About Me

Multimedia Design student based in Copenhagen, Denmark.

My name is Ion and I am from the Republic of Moldova. I have always been interested in working with technologies that boost my creativity. Recently, I started an amazing programme in Multimedia Design, which gives me the knowledge to grow in the Web Design field. This year I have mastered Photoshop, InDesign, XD, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, HTML, and CSS. Besides web design, I have a strong interest in digital marketing and e-commerce.
